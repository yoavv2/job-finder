## Decisions Made
- Skills are grouped `{ category?, items[] }` rather than a flat list, keeping groups addressable for tailoring reordering.
- Array sections default to `[]`; `experience` is required (may be an empty array) since it is the resume's core.
- Dates are opaque strings (no parsing), consistent with the config module's approach.
